What The Gallery Is

A museum-style part of the stop.

The gallery is where the visit slows down. Artwork, artifacts, and display pieces give the store a cultural and historical dimension.

It centers Shoshone and Arapaho people, including Eastern Shoshone and Northern Arapaho connections to the Wind River Reservation.
