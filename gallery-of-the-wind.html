Gallery of the Wind & Museum

The gallery and museum at Wind River Trading Company.

Public listings describe the Gallery of the Wind & Museum as a collection of artifacts and artwork of the Shoshone and Arapaho people. It sits at 14631 Hwy. 287 in Fort Washakie and uses the same phone number as Wind River Trading Company.

What The Gallery Is

The Gallery of the Wind & Museum is at the same address as the store.

Public reservation and tourism listings place the gallery at Wind River Trading Company on Highway 287 in Fort Washakie. It is part of the trading company property.

Those same public listings describe it as a collection of artifacts and artwork of the Shoshone and Arapaho people. Reservation-specific descriptions also name the Eastern Shoshone and Northern Arapaho tribes.
