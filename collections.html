What people come in for

People stop in for different things.

Some come for supplies. Some come for Pendleton. Some are shopping for gifts or finished work.

For makers Beads, ribbon, fabric by the yard, and beading supplies.
For Pendleton Blankets, clothing, and accessories.
For gifts Gifts, bags, wrap, and smaller items.
For finished work Beadwork, jewelry, and one-of-a-kind pieces.
