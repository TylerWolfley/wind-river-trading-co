About the store

A working store in Fort Washakie.

We're tied to Hines General Store, and we've been here since 1946. The store has stayed with the Hines family. People still use it every day.

History

Our roots go back to Hines General Store.

Wind River Trading Company grew out of Hines General Store and has served the Wind River Reservation since 1946.

Pendleton

Pendleton has long been part of the store.

We carry Pendleton blankets, clothing, and other items. The store is known as Wyoming's oldest Pendleton dealer.

Gallery

The gallery is part of the building.

The Gallery of the Wind & Museum has artwork and artifacts connected to Shoshone and Arapaho culture.

What we carry
