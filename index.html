Fort Washakie trading post

A trading post in Fort Washakie

Wind River Trading Company has been in Fort Washakie since 1946. People come in for Pendleton blankets, beadwork, Native-made goods, western wear, fabric, gifts, and the supplies they know they can find here.

Inside, the store opens into several rooms with jewelry, fabric, gifts, regional pieces, and beading supplies. The Gallery of the Wind & Museum is here too, so there’s usually more to look through than people expect from the road.
