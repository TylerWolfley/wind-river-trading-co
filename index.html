What You Will Find Inside

More than a small gift counter.

Inside the store you will find beading supplies, fabric, ribbon, jewelry, leatherwork, Pendleton, clothing, pottery, huckleberry foods, candles, soaps, toys, puzzles, and other gifts.

Beads / Fabric / Ribbon Supplies, fabric, and ribbon
Jewelry / Leatherwork Jewelry, bags, and leather goods
Pendleton / Apparel Blankets, clothing, and outerwear
Foods / Gifts Huckleberry foods, candles, soaps, toys, puzzles, and gifts

Store And Gallery

The gallery is at the same address as the store.

Wind River Trading Company and the Gallery of the Wind & Museum share the same address and phone number on Highway 287 in Fort Washakie.
