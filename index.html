Fort Washakie trading post

Rooted in Fort Washakie

Wind River Trading Company is a working trading post in Fort Washakie, known for Native-made goods, Pendleton blankets, beadwork, and western wear. The store draws both locals and travelers moving through the Wind River Reservation, offering a mix of practical supplies and handmade pieces that are hard to find elsewhere.

Inside, the store opens up into multiple rooms filled with jewelry, fabrics, gifts, and regional items, alongside the Gallery of the Wind & Museum. It’s a place people come to browse, find something specific, or spend time looking through work tied to the area.
