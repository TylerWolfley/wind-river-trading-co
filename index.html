Store and gallery

The gallery changes the visit.

The trading post side handles the goods and supplies; the Gallery of the Wind & Museum adds artwork and artifacts that make the building feel larger than retail.

Gallery Artwork and artifacts connected to Shoshone and Arapaho culture give the building a quieter museum side.
