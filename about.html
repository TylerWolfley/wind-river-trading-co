About Wind River Trading Company

A trading post and gift shop on Highway 287.

Wind River Trading Company is a store on Highway 287 in Fort Washakie. People stop in for beads, fabric, ribbon, jewelry, leatherwork, Pendleton, clothing, pottery, huckleberry foods, candles, soaps, toys, puzzles, and the Gallery of the Wind & Museum on the same property.

What Wind River Trading Company Is

A full store, not a small checkout counter.

This is a full store with clothing, blankets, display cases, gifts, and beading supplies. People stop here because there is more to look through than a few shelves by the register.

The store carries beads, fabric, ribbon, jewelry, leatherwork, Pendleton, clothing, pottery, huckleberry foods, candles, soaps, toys, puzzles, artwork, rugs, drums, and artifacts.

Supplies Beads, fabric, ribbon, and other supplies people come in to buy.
Clothing and goods Jewelry, leatherwork, Pendleton, clothing, and bags are a big part of the store.
Food and gifts Huckleberry foods, candles, soaps, toys, puzzles, and other gifts fill out the shelves.
