A few basics

A few dates.

The basics are simple. The store has been here a long time and it's still in use.

1946 The business traces back to Hines General Store serving the Wind River Reservation.
1978 A major buildout created today's Wind River Trading Company beside the original store.
Family The store has stayed with the Hines family for generations.
Pendleton The gift shop remains Wyoming's oldest Pendleton dealer.

Still a store

This is still a working trading post.

People come in for Pendleton, locally made beadwork and jewelry, fabric by the yard, gifts, and beading supplies.

The Gallery of the Wind & Museum is part of the building too.

Store floor Materials, finished work, clothing, and gifts are all part of the store.
Local work Locally made items and one-of-a-kind pieces are part of what people come in for.
Gallery The gallery has artwork and artifacts connected to Shoshone and Arapaho culture.
