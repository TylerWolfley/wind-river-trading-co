About Wind River Trading Company

A Fort Washakie trading company and highway stop on US 287.

Wind River Trading Company is a long-running stop at 14631 Hwy. 287 in Fort Washakie. Shoppers come in for beads, fabric, ribbon, jewelry, leatherwork, Pendleton, apparel, pottery, huckleberry foods, candles, soaps, toys, puzzles, and the Gallery of the Wind & Museum on the same property.

What Wind River Trading Company Is

A browseable trading company with a broad floor mix.

This is not a one-case souvenir counter. The building is set up for people to move through apparel, blankets, shelves, display cases, gift merchandise, and supply items in one pass.

Across the floor, the mix runs from beads, fabric, ribbon, jewelry, leatherwork, Pendleton, apparel, pottery, huckleberry foods, candles, soaps, toys, and puzzles to Native American arts, crafts, beadwork, rugs, drums, and artifacts.

Supplies Beads, fabric, ribbon, and other materials that make the store useful as well as browseable.
Wear and carry Jewelry, leatherwork, Pendleton, apparel, bags, and other pieces that give the floor its strongest trading-post feel.
Gift and food shelves Huckleberry foods, candles, soaps, toys, puzzles, and smaller shelves that keep the stop from feeling narrow.
