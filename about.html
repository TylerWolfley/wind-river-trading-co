About

From Hines General Store to Wind River Trading Company.

The business has served the Wind River Reservation since 1946 and has stayed in the Hines family.

Store history

The current store grew beside the old one.

The business began with Hines General Store. A major buildout in 1978 created today's Wind River Trading Company beside the original store.

The store has stayed in the Hines family.

The short version.

A few points cover the main history.

1946 Hines General Store was serving the Wind River Reservation by 1946.
1978 The current Wind River Trading Company building took shape beside the original store.
Family The store has stayed with the Hines family for generations.
Today Still a working trading post in Fort Washakie.

Still open

This is still a working trading post.

People still come in here for Pendleton, finished beadwork and jewelry, fabric by the yard, gifts, and beading supplies.

The gallery and museum remain part of the building.
