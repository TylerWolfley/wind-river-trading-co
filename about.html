Since 1946 in Fort Washakie

A Fort Washakie trading post with a long memory.

Since 1946, the store has stayed practical: Pendleton blankets, apparel, accessories, beadwork, jewelry, t-shirts, gifts, fabric by the yard, gift wrap, beading supplies, and a gallery under one roof.

The 1946 thread

Rooted in a longer Fort Washakie story.

The story reaches back to Hines General Store, a Fort Washakie store and trading post that County 10 reports has served the Wind River Reservation since 1946. The same piece notes that a 1978 construction project helped create today's Wind River Trading Company.

The Wind River Visitors Council also lists Wind River Trading Company with the Gallery of the Wind & Museum. That matches what visitors find in the building today.
