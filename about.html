Rooted in Fort Washakie

History still sits inside the place.

Wind River Trading Company grew out of Hines General Store, which has served the Wind River Reservation since 1946. A major expansion in 1978 created the present-day trading company beside the original store, and the business has remained in the Hines family ever since.

Today it is still a working part of Fort Washakie, with deep local roots and a long place in the life of the community.
